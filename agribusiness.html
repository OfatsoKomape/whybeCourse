Agribusiness Manager Career Path

Bridging farming and commerce in South Africa's food value chain

Career Progression Timeline

Junior Analyst (0-2 years)

R250K - R320K

Typical Roles:

  • Market research analyst at GrainSA or Hortgro
  • Export documentation assistant for citrus/deciduous fruit
  • Procurement assistant at agricultural cooperatives
  • Farm financial record keeper

Key Skills to Develop:

Agri-Excel Commodity Markets Export Regulations Farm Management Software

SA Certifications:

  • Perishable Products Export Control Board (PPECB) certification
  • Agricultural Risk Management short course (UFS)

Supply Chain Coordinator (3-5 years)

R350K - R500K

Advancement Opportunities:

  • Managing fruit export logistics to EU/China
  • Farm procurement specialist (machinery/inputs)
  • Agricultural commodity trader at JSE
  • Cooperative branch manager

Career Boosters:

  • Learn Chinese/Russian for export markets
  • Specialize in climate-smart supply chains
  • Get certified in blockchain for agri-traceability
