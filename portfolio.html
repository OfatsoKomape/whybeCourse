Portfolio Formats

Digital Portfolio

Professional website showcasing your work. Ideal for most creative and technical fields.

Best for: Designers, Developers, Creatives

Platforms: WordPress, Webflow, Adobe Portfolio

PDF Portfolio

Downloadable document perfect for email attachments or printed materials.

Best for: Architects, Consultants, Academics

Tools: InDesign, Canva, Google Slides

Video Portfolio

Dynamic presentation of your work, ideal for motion-based professions.

Best for: Animators, Filmmakers, Presenters

Platforms: Vimeo, YouTube, personal website

Field-Specific Guidance

Design & Creative

  • Showcase your visual aesthetic and style
  • Balance client work with personal projects
  • Demonstrate versatility while showing specialization
  • Include case studies with before/after examples

Development & IT

  • Link to live projects and GitHub repositories
  • Document technical challenges and solutions
  • Include clean, commented code samples
  • Show architecture diagrams and system designs

Business & Finance

  • Present reports and analyses with visual data
  • Quantify your impact with metrics and KPIs
  • Include professional presentations
  • Showcase strategic recommendations
