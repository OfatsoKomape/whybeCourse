What South African employers are looking for in 2023 and beyond

The Changing Skills Landscape in South Africa

Based on analysis of 50,000+ South African job postings and employer surveys, these are the most sought-after skills across industries. The Fourth Industrial Revolution and digital transformation are reshaping what skills are valuable in the job market.

Top In-Demand Skills

92% Demand

Key Components:

Microsoft Office Email Communication Basic Cybersecurity Cloud Storage Social Media Online Collaboration

How to Develop These Skills

South African students and job seekers can acquire these valuable skills through:

  • Online courses: Coursera, Udemy, Udacity, and local platforms like HyperionDev
  • Free resources: YouTube tutorials, freeCodeCamp, Khan Academy, W3Schools
  • University programs: Many SA universities now offer specialized courses in these areas
  • Internships: Gain practical experience while learning
  • Certifications: Microsoft, Google, AWS, and other industry-recognized certifications
  • Student organizations: Join tech clubs, debate teams, or entrepreneurship societies
  • Volunteering: Gain real-world experience while contributing to causes
  • Personal projects: Build apps, websites, or start small businesses to practice skills
