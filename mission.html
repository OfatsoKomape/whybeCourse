The South African Challenge

In South Africa, 60% of high school students graduate unsure of their career path, while youth unemployment sits at a staggering 45%. This isn’t just a statistic, it’s thousands of talented young people missing opportunities to build fulfilling lives.

The disconnect is clear: many students choose degrees without understanding job market demands, while growing industries struggle to find skilled local talent. At whybeCourse, we’re changing this by:

  • Matching passions to demand: Showing which careers align with South Africa’s growing sectors (renewable energy, tech, healthcare).
  • Demystifying education paths: Revealing which qualifications actually lead to employment in their chosen field.
  • Highlighting hidden opportunities: Spotlighting less-saturated careers with high growth potential in SA.

We’re not just guiding students, we’re helping build a stronger South African economy one informed career choice at a time.
